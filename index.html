About me:
I received my MFA in Electronic and Time Based Art from Purdue University in 2019 and am currently an Assistant Professor at the University of Southern Maine. My work explores how deconstructing everyday technologies, or even designing them yourself can question larger oppressive systems and create a space for participatory action. I am currently researching the critical possibilities for Art and AI at the Michael E. Dubyak Center for Digital Science and Innovation. Contact me!
